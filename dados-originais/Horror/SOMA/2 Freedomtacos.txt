First 10 minutes: Hey this looks pretty cool! Simon is from Toronto just like I am!

First 30 minutes: Oh ♥♥♥♥, this just got awesome.

1 hour later: This is like if Rapture was taken over by sentient synths.

3 hours later: Jesus christ, is this really happening?!

2nd half and on: WHAT THE FUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUCK IS GOING ON???!???!

Epilogue: I am a broken man.

9/10 - One of the most incredible psychological horror games I've ever played.
