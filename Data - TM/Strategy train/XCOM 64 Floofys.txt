How do you like your strategy games? If you like character permadeath and careful planning of equipment, squad deployment, training, management, and all that, you'll like XCOM.

You've got all sorts of things to take into account before going into a fight, and you'll have to carefully manage your troops once the game begins.

You'll never forget your first 95% chance to hit that you lined up perfectly, and absolutely needed to hit for you to execute your plan, and you miss.
